# Participant Consent Form (Draft)

## Purpose
You are being invited to participate in a research project exploring how opportunity constraints and development inputs affect football prospect evaluation.

## What Data Is Collected
- Football participation and performance information
- Training, coaching, and development inputs
- Time and opportunity constraints related to football participation

No medical, financial, or sensitive personal information is collected.

## How Data Is Used
Data is used to study patterns in football development and evaluation. Results are used for research and educational purposes only.

## Privacy
All participant data is anonymized for analysis. Names and identifying information are not included in modeling datasets.

## Voluntary Participation
Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty, and your data will be removed from future analysis upon request.

## No Guarantees
Participation does not guarantee recruiting exposure, offers, or athletic advancement.
